<span

preprocessor）。它的基本思想是，用一种专门的编程语言，进行网页样式设计，然后再编译成正常的CSS文件。</span>

</div>

<div

</div>

<span
style="font-family: &quot;Verdana&quot;; font-weight: bold;">一、什么是SASS</span>

</div>

<div

<span

</div>

<div
style="color: black; direction: ltr; font-family: &quot;Arial&quot;; font-size: 11pt; margin-bottom: 0; margin-left: 7.5pt; margin-right: 7.5pt; margin-top: 0; padding: 0;">

</div>

<div
style="color: black; direction: ltr; font-family: &quot;Arial&quot;; font-size: 11pt; margin-bottom: 0; margin-left: 7.5pt; margin-right: 7.5pt; margin-top: 0; padding: 0;">
