<span
style="font-family: &quot;Verdana&quot;;">以前，我写过一篇</span><span
style="color: #0000ee; font-family: &quot;Verdana&quot;; text-decoration: underline;">[《如何面试程序员》](http://www.ruanyifeng.com/blog/2010/12/how_to_interview_a_programmer.html)</span><span
style="font-family: &quot;Verdana&quot;;">，探讨公司如何向应聘者提问。</span>

</div>

<div
style="color: black; direction: ltr; font-family: &quot;Arial&quot;; font-size: 11pt; margin-bottom: 0; margin-left: 7.5pt; margin-right: 7.5pt; margin-top: 0; padding: 0;">

<span
style="font-family: &quot;Verdana&quot;;">今天，我看到硅谷招聘经理</span><span
style="color: #0000ee; font-family: &quot;Verdana&quot;; text-decoration: underline;">[Steve
Buckley](http://voltsteve.blogspot.com/2011/12/assessing-company-questions-you-need-to.html)</span><span
style="font-family: &quot;Verdana&quot;;">的一篇文章，正好探讨了同一件事的另一面：应聘者如何向公司提问。</span>

</div>

<div
style="color: black; direction: ltr; font-family: &quot;Arial&quot;; font-size: 11pt; margin-bottom: 0; margin-left: 7.5pt; margin-right: 7.5pt; margin-top: 0; padding: 0;">

![](https://lh4.googleusercontent.com/bx516ThXTtP5sTrgk_Vz7x-9KlNu5sMsniw5qCXujkO_hYL3M4-WcUY1fK1I4F4nCJbo8BNOdjEO4iEhXkhAlGnfboZnEh8ZEvHp8cHoQk0MECFYqaQ){width="600"
height="439"}

</div>

<div
style="color: black; direction: ltr; font-family: &quot;Arial&quot;; font-size: 11pt; margin-bottom: 0; margin-left: 7.5pt; margin-right: 7.5pt; margin-top: 0; padding: 0;">

<span
style="font-family: &quot;Verdana&quot;; font-weight: bold;">很多人将面试看作一种单向选择，事实上，面试是一种双向选择：不仅是公司挑选你，也是你挑选公司。</span><span
style="font-family: &quot;Verdana&quot;;">面试就是为双方提供互相了解的机会，公司在评估你，你也在评估公司。</span>

</div>

<div
style="color: black; direction: ltr; font-family: &quot;Arial&quot;; font-size: 11pt; margin-bottom: 0; margin-left: 7.5pt; margin-right: 7.5pt; margin-top: 0; padding: 0;">

<span
style="font-family: &quot;Verdana&quot;;">面试官也知道这一点，所以他们有心理准备，期待你提出问题，并且会做出回答。所以，面试时不要浪费向公司提问的机会。而且，你主动提问，表明你比较重视这个职位，会加深面试官对你的印象，可能会提高面试的成功率。</span>

</div>

<div
style="color: black; direction: ltr; font-family: &quot;Arial&quot;; font-size: 11pt; margin-bottom: 0; margin-left: 7.5pt; margin-right: 7.5pt; margin-top: 0; padding: 0;">

![](https://lh6.googleusercontent.com/Cp2rbPoJDb8c_Hf375LpbNdbNrDJ1dRgeaGjoLJgz7--k0Rk78_RJPoVE3A64lIURC0KiHlPW5Po0BjA5DBEKZ9ot1B23B4aI_S-wNYCZ36DuP2-Xpg){width="600"
height="420"}

</div>

<div
style="color: black; direction: ltr; font-family: &quot;Arial&quot;; font-size: 11pt; margin-bottom: 0; margin-left: 7.5pt; margin-right: 7.5pt; margin-top: 0; padding: 0;">

<span
style="font-family: &quot;Verdana&quot;;">有一些注意点，你需要知道：</span>

</div>

<div
style="color: black; direction: ltr; font-family: &quot;Arial&quot;; font-size: 11pt; margin-bottom: 0; margin-left: 7.5pt; margin-right: 7.5pt; margin-top: 0; padding: 0;">

<span style="font-family: &quot;Verdana&quot;;">1.
面试之前，一定要做准备，多了解公司的情况。</span>

</div>

<div
style="color: black; direction: ltr; font-family: &quot;Arial&quot;; font-size: 11pt; margin-bottom: 0; margin-left: 7.5pt; margin-right: 7.5pt; margin-top: 0; padding: 0;">

<span style="font-family: &quot;Verdana&quot;;">2.
你提出的问题，应该围绕"这份工作是否合适我"这个中心点，其他与应聘关系不大的问题，不宜多问。</span>

</div>

<div
style="color: black; direction: ltr; font-family: &quot;Arial&quot;; font-size: 11pt; margin-bottom: 0; margin-left: 7.5pt; margin-right: 7.5pt; margin-top: 0; padding: 0;">

<span style="font-family: &quot;Verdana&quot;;">3.
提问的时候，要自然放松，不要害羞，就把它当作普通的聊天。你要表现出对公司的真诚兴趣。</span>

</div>

<div
style="color: black; direction: ltr; font-family: &quot;Arial&quot;; font-size: 11pt; margin-bottom: 0; margin-left: 7.5pt; margin-right: 7.5pt; margin-top: 0; padding: 0;">

<span style="font-family: &quot;Verdana&quot;;">4.
提问要直接了当，不要绕圈子。提出问题之后，你要保持安静，让面试官多说话。</span>

</div>

<div
style="color: black; direction: ltr; font-family: &quot;Arial&quot;; font-size: 11pt; margin-bottom: 0; margin-left: 7.5pt; margin-right: 7.5pt; margin-top: 0; padding: 0;">

<span style="font-family: &quot;Verdana&quot;;">5.
面试官回答的时候，你可以做笔记，或者事先询问能不能做。笔记必须简短，你的大部分时间，要用来全神贯注倾听面试官的回答，并与其有眼神的交流。</span>

</div>

<div
style="color: black; direction: ltr; font-family: &quot;Arial&quot;; font-size: 11pt; margin-bottom: 0; margin-left: 7.5pt; margin-right: 7.5pt; margin-top: 0; padding: 0;">

<span style="font-family: &quot;Verdana&quot;;">6.
面试结束后一周内，最好打一个电话或发一封邮件，了解公司对你的反馈意见。即使面试失败，你不妨也问一下原因，这会有助于你以后的面试。</span>

</div>

<div
style="color: black; direction: ltr; font-family: &quot;Arial&quot;; font-size: 11pt; margin-bottom: 0; margin-left: 7.5pt; margin-right: 7.5pt; margin-top: 0; padding: 0;">

<span
style="font-family: &quot;Verdana&quot;;">下面是一些你可以问的典型问题。</span>

</div>

<div
style="color: black; direction: ltr; font-family: &quot;Arial&quot;; font-size: 11pt; margin-bottom: 0; margin-left: 7.5pt; margin-right: 7.5pt; margin-top: 0; padding: 0;">

<span
style="font-family: &quot;Verdana&quot;; font-weight: bold;">问题一：你们为什么要招聘这个职位？</span>

</div>

<div
style="color: black; direction: ltr; font-family: &quot;Arial&quot;; font-size: 11pt; margin-bottom: 0; margin-left: 7.5pt; margin-right: 7.5pt; margin-top: 0; padding: 0;">

<span style="font-family: &quot;Verdana&quot;;">Q1: Why are you
currently recruiting for this position?</span>

</div>

<div
style="color: black; direction: ltr; font-family: &quot;Arial&quot;; font-size: 11pt; margin-bottom: 0; margin-left: 7.5pt; margin-right: 7.5pt; margin-top: 0; padding: 0;">

<span
style="font-family: &quot;Verdana&quot;;">这个问题会使得面试官开始谈论当前的项目，或者谈论前一位离职人员。无论哪种情况，都会让你了解，一些与你最密切相关的公司情况。</span>

</div>

<div
style="color: black; direction: ltr; font-family: &quot;Arial&quot;; font-size: 11pt; margin-bottom: 0; margin-left: 7.5pt; margin-right: 7.5pt; margin-top: 0; padding: 0;">

<span
style="font-family: &quot;Verdana&quot;; font-weight: bold;">问题二：你们的新员工多吗？</span>

</div>

<div
style="color: black; direction: ltr; font-family: &quot;Arial&quot;; font-size: 11pt; margin-bottom: 0; margin-left: 7.5pt; margin-right: 7.5pt; margin-top: 0; padding: 0;">

<span style="font-family: &quot;Verdana&quot;;">Q2: Do you have many new
staffs?</span>

</div>

<div
style="color: black; direction: ltr; font-family: &quot;Arial&quot;; font-size: 11pt; margin-bottom: 0; margin-left: 7.5pt; margin-right: 7.5pt; margin-top: 0; padding: 0;">

<span
style="font-family: &quot;Verdana&quot;;">这个问题起一个过渡作用，使得谈话导向公司内部的情况。但是，它本身也能说明一些问题。如果公司成立已经超过四年，又没有新项目，但是新员工却很多，这往往说明公司文化不是很健康。</span>

</div>

<div
style="color: black; direction: ltr; font-family: &quot;Arial&quot;; font-size: 11pt; margin-bottom: 0; margin-left: 7.5pt; margin-right: 7.5pt; margin-top: 0; padding: 0;">

<span
style="font-family: &quot;Verdana&quot;; font-weight: bold;">问题三：你们公司（团队）目前面临的最大挑战是什么？</span>

</div>

<div
style="color: black; direction: ltr; font-family: &quot;Arial&quot;; font-size: 11pt; margin-bottom: 0; margin-left: 7.5pt; margin-right: 7.5pt; margin-top: 0; padding: 0;">

<span style="font-family: &quot;Verdana&quot;;">Q3: What are the biggest
challenges your team are facing right now?</span>

</div>

<div
style="color: black; direction: ltr; font-family: &quot;Arial&quot;; font-size: 11pt; margin-bottom: 0; margin-left: 7.5pt; margin-right: 7.5pt; margin-top: 0; padding: 0;">

<span
style="font-family: &quot;Verdana&quot;;">如果面试官开始谈论一些具体的技术问题，这很好；如果他的回答是项目时间紧迫，或者需要更多的资金，那你就要小心一点了，公司管理上面可能有问题。</span>

</div>

<div
style="color: black; direction: ltr; font-family: &quot;Arial&quot;; font-size: 11pt; margin-bottom: 0; margin-left: 7.5pt; margin-right: 7.5pt; margin-top: 0; padding: 0;">

<span
style="font-family: &quot;Verdana&quot;; font-weight: bold;">问题四：什么新技术（编程语言）是你们未来希望采用的？</span>

</div>

<div
style="color: black; direction: ltr; font-family: &quot;Arial&quot;; font-size: 11pt; margin-bottom: 0; margin-left: 7.5pt; margin-right: 7.5pt; margin-top: 0; padding: 0;">

<span style="font-family: &quot;Verdana&quot;;">Q4: What
technologies/languages would you like to see your team adapt to that
aren't currently being utilised?</span>

</div>

<div
style="color: black; direction: ltr; font-family: &quot;Arial&quot;; font-size: 11pt; margin-bottom: 0; margin-left: 7.5pt; margin-right: 7.5pt; margin-top: 0; padding: 0;">

<span
style="font-family: &quot;Verdana&quot;;">如果你申请的是技术职位，面试官恰巧又是技术负责人，那么这个问题将会非常合适。你会对公司的技术路线有所了解和准备，一旦入职，就能更好地适应公司的需要。</span>

</div>

<div
style="color: black; direction: ltr; font-family: &quot;Arial&quot;; font-size: 11pt; margin-bottom: 0; margin-left: 7.5pt; margin-right: 7.5pt; margin-top: 0; padding: 0;">

<span
style="font-family: &quot;Verdana&quot;; font-weight: bold;">问题五：在业务方面，有没有什么地方是你们不满意的，未来想要改进的？</span>

</div>

<div
style="color: black; direction: ltr; font-family: &quot;Arial&quot;; font-size: 11pt; margin-bottom: 0; margin-left: 7.5pt; margin-right: 7.5pt; margin-top: 0; padding: 0;">

<span style="font-family: &quot;Verdana&quot;;">Q5: Few companies, if
any, are 100% satisfied with the way their business is operating. If you
could simply flick a switch to fix it, what one thing would you
change?</span>

</div>

<div
style="color: black; direction: ltr; font-family: &quot;Arial&quot;; font-size: 11pt; margin-bottom: 0; margin-left: 7.5pt; margin-right: 7.5pt; margin-top: 0; padding: 0;">

<span
style="font-family: &quot;Verdana&quot;;">很少有公司，会百分之百满意自身的现状，即使那些状况很良好的公司也是如此。这个问题可以让你对公司管理层的关注重点和担忧之处，有所了解。</span>

</div>

<div
style="color: black; direction: ltr; font-family: &quot;Arial&quot;; font-size: 11pt; margin-bottom: 0; margin-left: 7.5pt; margin-right: 7.5pt; margin-top: 0; padding: 0;">

<span
style="font-family: &quot;Verdana&quot;; font-weight: bold;">问题六：我申请的这个职位，对公司的业务有何影响？</span>

</div>

<div
style="color: black; direction: ltr; font-family: &quot;Arial&quot;; font-size: 11pt; margin-bottom: 0; margin-left: 7.5pt; margin-right: 7.5pt; margin-top: 0; padding: 0;">

<span style="font-family: &quot;Verdana&quot;;">Q6: If you struggle to
fill the position I have applied for, what impact would that have on the
business?</span>

</div>

<div
style="color: black; direction: ltr; font-family: &quot;Arial&quot;; font-size: 11pt; margin-bottom: 0; margin-left: 7.5pt; margin-right: 7.5pt; margin-top: 0; padding: 0;">

<span
style="font-family: &quot;Verdana&quot;;">这个问题会让你了解自己在公司的角色，以及你的岗位对公司是否重要。</span>

</div>

<div
style="color: black; direction: ltr; font-family: &quot;Arial&quot;; font-size: 11pt; margin-bottom: 0; margin-left: 7.5pt; margin-right: 7.5pt; margin-top: 0; padding-bottom: 16pt; padding-left: 0; padding-right: 0; padding-top: 0;">

<span style="font-family: &quot;Verdana&quot;;">（完）</span>

</div>
